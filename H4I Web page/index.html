Our Work

We collaborate with nonprofits and other socially responsible organizations to develop software that meets important social and humanitarian needs.

Projects

Last semester, we worked with two nonprofits: Life of Freedom Center and HAART Kenya. We have developed data visuals to help the nonprofits find trends in victim data and fight human trafficking. This semester, we have more exciting opportunities to learn web design and mobile app development!
